# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful environment for everyone contributing to or using this project.

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment‑free experience for all, regardless of:
- age  
- body size  
- disability  
- ethnicity  
- gender identity or expression  
- level of experience  
- nationality  
- personal appearance  
- race  
- religion  
- sexual identity or orientation  

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of positive behavior:
- Being respectful and considerate  
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  
- Showing empathy toward others  

### Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or discriminatory comments  
- Public or private intimidation  
- Publishing others’ private information  
- Disruptive or aggressive behavior  
- Any conduct that violates applicable laws  

---

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct.  
They may take appropriate action, including warnings or bans, in response to unacceptable behavior.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- all project spaces (GitHub issues, PRs, discussions)  
- public spaces where the project is represented  

---

## Reporting Issues
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it privately:

📧 **contact@bkresearchandanalytics.com**

Reports will be handled confidentially and addressed within a reasonable timeframe.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction  
For minor issues, maintainers may provide a gentle reminder of expected behavior.

### 2. Warning  
For more serious or repeated violations, a formal warning may be issued.

### 3. Temporary Ban  
For continued violations, contributors may be temporarily removed from community spaces.

### 4. Permanent Ban  
For severe or repeated misconduct, a permanent ban may be applied.
